# Job Match Platform - User Manual

## Overview
Job Match Platform is an AI-powered job matching system that connects job seekers with relevant opportunities. It uses advanced NLP to parse resumes, extract skills, and match candidates with jobs.

## Getting Started

### Registration
1. Navigate to the platform URL
2. Click "Register" button
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, number, special character)
4. Verify your email via the link sent to your inbox
5. Log in with your credentials

### Profile Setup
1. Go to "My Profile" section
2. Upload your resume (PDF, DOCX, or TXT format)
3. The system will automatically parse your resume and extract:
   - Skills
   - Work Experience
   - Education
   - Projects
4. Review and edit extracted information
5. Add any additional skills manually

### Job Search
1. Use the search bar to find jobs by keyword, title, or company
2. Filter results by:
   - Location (remote, hybrid, on-site)
   - Job type (full-time, part-time, contract)
   - Experience level
   - Salary range
3. View job details by clicking on any listing
4. See your match score for each position

### Skill Gap Analysis
For each job posting, the system provides:
- **Skills Matched**: Skills from your resume that match the job requirements
- **Skills Missing**: Skills required by the job but not found in your resume
- **Match Rate**: Percentage of required skills you possess
- **Learning Suggestions**: Recommended courses and resources
- **Certification Recommendations**: Industry-recognized certifications

### Applying to Jobs
1. Click "Apply" on any job listing
2. Your resume is automatically attached
3. Track application status in "My Applications"

### Saved Jobs
1. Click the bookmark icon to save jobs for later
2. Access saved jobs from "Saved Jobs" section
3. Apply directly from saved jobs

### Recommendations
The platform provides job recommendations based on:
- **Collaborative Filtering**: What similar users have applied to
- **Content-Based Filtering**: Match between your profile and job descriptions
- **Hybrid Recommendations**: Combined approach for best results

### Account Settings
- **Password**: Change password in settings
- **Notifications**: Manage email notification preferences
- **Data Privacy**: Request data export or account deletion (GDPR compliant)

## Troubleshooting

### Resume Upload Issues
- Accepted formats: PDF, DOCX, TXT
- Maximum file size: 10MB
- Ensure the file is not password protected

### Login Problems
- Use "Forgot Password" to reset
- Account may be temporarily locked after 5 failed attempts
- Wait 15 minutes before trying again

### Match Score Too Low
- Update your resume with current skills
- Add relevant certifications
- Complete your profile with more detail

## FAQ

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We comply with GDPR.

**Q: How often are recommendations updated?**
A: Recommendations are updated in real-time based on your activity.

**Q: Can I upload multiple resumes?**
A: You can maintain one active resume. Previous versions are archived.
